Below is a couple fun facts. These facts are not about me but I did think they were interesting! 1.Honey never spoils. 2.Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are still edible after thousands of years. 3.The world's largest snowflake on record was 15 inches wide and 8 inches thick, falling in Montana in 1887. 4.The shortest war in history lasted only 38 minutes between the countries of Zanzibar and Great Britain in 1896. 5.Kangaroos cannot walk backwards and their tail helps them balance while jumping. 6.The Eiffel Tower was originally meant to be a temporary structure and was almost dismantled after the World Fair of 1889. 7.A group of flamingos is called a flamboyance. 8.There is a species of jellyfish that is immortal and can potentially live forever. 9.The tallest mountain on Earth, Mount Everest, is still growing taller by a few millimeters each year due to tectonic plate movement. 10.An octopus has three hearts and blue blood. 11.There are more possible iterations of a game of chess than there are atoms in the known universe.
